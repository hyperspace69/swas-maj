Swastik Majumder

Physics PhD student working in statistical physics and field theory.

I am Swastik Majumder from Kolkata, West Bengal, India. My research interests lie in theoretical physics, especially statistical physics and field theory. I am interested in how complex and emergent behavior arises in interacting many-body systems, disordered geometries, and stochastic models.

Beyond physics, I am an avid reader of non-fiction, philosophy, history, and especially the history of politics. I also write poetry, blog about books I have been reading, enjoy teaching physics, and closely follow cricket.
